            <li><strong>Depth:</strong> 1 point for demonstrating a deeper understanding through examples or elaboration.</li>
        </ul>

            <li><strong>Edge Cases:</strong> 1 point for handling edge cases effectively.</li>
            <li><strong>Code Clarity:</strong> 1 point for clean, well-documented code.</li>
        </ul>

        </ul>
